About

Hi, I'm Kai.

I am a third-year student at Boston University, studying Computer Science. I have experience in developing software for the web and native apps for Android devices - from static pages to full-stack applications.

I've been learning Android development outside of my classes since the fall of '16, and I love it; I enjoy the framework, and being able to take my code with me/my phone wherever I go is extremely gratifying. I'm always looking to create apps that solve some real-world problem, no matter how small, to make life easier for either myself or the people around me.

Whenever I can, I enjoy hanging out on a beach, jamming out on my ukulele, and playing video games.
